Policies

The Kyper for President Campaign looks to bring strength and unity back to the United States.

-We want to include all perspectives in the deal making process in order to do what is best for the American people.
-We want to base our principles on compromise, because one set mind-set and ideology creates division, not unity in the law

-We look to bring strength and stability to the Middle Class of America by:
     -Making the rich pay their fair share of taxes
     -Since the 2008 recession, the top one percent has seen the most economic growth
     -Close tax loopholes and simplify the extensiveness of deductions and write-offs, so the top one percent and the      corporations of America re-invest in the middle class
     -Lowering taxes on the middle class, relieving economic pressure. The more money that the middle class is able to      spend, the more money can be spent for circulation in the economy, creating an economic stimulation that benefits      every single American
     -Bringing jobs back to the work force of America       -The government has been creating jobs, but desk and      office jobs. We need to create working jobs that re-invest in America.

-Infrastructure:
      -We want to rebuild the infrastructure of the United States
      -The reconstruction of railways and motorways creates tremendous amounts of jobs that stimulate the economy,      and solidify transportation in the country.

-Government Programs:
     -We look to use the excess revenue gained from closing tax loopholes to replenish the dwindling Medicare and      Social Security programs, so that you can have security in your future.

Overall, our policies reflect our main goal to support the burdened middle class, and the Kyper for President Campaign fully believes we can do just that.
