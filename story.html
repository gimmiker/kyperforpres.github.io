Kyper's Story

-Attended Penn State University -Worked in management position in private sector for seven years
-Went back to school for teaching certification
-Taught at a prison for two years
-Taught at a juvenile detention center for five years
-Has taught at Lower Dauphin High School for fourteen years
-Currently teaches Advanced Placement United States Government and Ethics
Our campaign believes that Mr. Kyper is completely qualified to be the next president of the United States. Having worked in both the public and private sectors, he fully understands what it takes to make compromises and deals that benefit the people. Most importantly, Mr. Kyper's teaching career has given great experience that will transition over to the White House. Working with both troubled youths and bright young students, he knows how to communicate and achieve the highest potential that each individual has within themselves. Our campaign believes in compromise and the inclusion of many perspectives, not one, to do what is best for this country, and Mr. Kyper's entire career has been doing just that.
